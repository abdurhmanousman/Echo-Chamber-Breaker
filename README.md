#⚖️ Echo-Chamber Breaker: The Strategic Research Swarm
Gemini Nexus: The Agentverse // Build with AI Hackathon Submission
Track: A — The Intelligence Bureau (Strategic Research Swarm)

SDG Alignment: Goal 16 — Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions

📖 Project Overview
Echo-Chamber Breaker is an autonomous multi-agent research system designed to combat algorithmic bias and digital polarization. While traditional search engines and AI often reinforce a user's existing beliefs (Confirmation Bias), our "Swarm" is programmed to be intentionally adversarial—seeking out credible dissenting data to provide a 360-degree view of any complex topic.

The Problem
Digital echo chambers fragment public discourse, making objective research for policy-making and journalism nearly impossible. This polarization undermines the "Strong Institutions" required for SDG 16.

The Solution
Using Gemini 3 Flash, we’ve built a coordinated swarm of agents that use different "Thinking Levels" to scout, challenge, and synthesize information, ensuring users aren't just hearing what they want to hear.

🛠️ How it Works (Track A Architecture)
We utilize a three-stage agent orchestration flow:

The Scout (Low Thinking): Rapidly identifies the mainstream narrative using Google Search Grounding.

The Dissenter (High Thinking): Uses Gemini 3's advanced reasoning to perform deep-dive "Red Team" research, specifically looking for credible counter-arguments and data gaps.

The Synthesizer (Medium Thinking): Acts as the objective judge, weighing both sides to produce a Balanced Intelligence Briefing.


🚀 Key Features
Adversarial Reasoning: Unlike standard chatbots, our agents are prompted to disagree, uncovering "hidden" truths.

Gemini 3 Thinking Levels: We optimize compute costs and reasoning depth by assigning low, medium, and high thinking configs based on the agent's task complexity.

Fact-Grounded: Every report includes citations directly from Google Search metadata to ensure transparency and accountability.
